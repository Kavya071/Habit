## ğŸ­ Animation Patterns

### On Scroll:
- Sections fade in from bottom (30px offset)
- Cards stagger with 0.1s delay between each
- Stats count up animation
- Progress bars fill animation

### On Hover:
- Cards lift up 10px with shadow increase
- Buttons scale to 1.05x
- Icons rotate or change color
- Borders appear/change color

### Background:
- Gradient orbs float slowly (20s loops)
- Phone mockup has subtle rotation
- Floating cards move in sine waves
